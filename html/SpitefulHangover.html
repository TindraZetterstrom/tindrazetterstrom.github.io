description/tag line: Show the undead what it means to have a spiteful hangover.

"You wake up with a brutal hangover, angry that the spiteful undead have ruined your village. Drink, fight, and brawl your way through Viking madness to crush the dark force ruining your buzz." Spiteful Hangover was made over the course of 13 weeks by 13 students known as the Rat Bastards as part of their 5th project at The Game Assembly - Stockholm along with 4 students from Audio Production Academy. Petronella Jonasson (Character Artist) Siri Hellman (Game Artist) Ilse Hermans (Level Designer) Daniel Gunnarsson (Level Designer) Gustav Eriksson (Level Designer) Niklas Malmrot (Technical Procedural Artist) Robin Ekström (Procedural Sim & Material Artist) William Melander (Programmer) Emilio Brandhild (Programmer) Jacob van Berlo (Programmer) Simon Östman (Programmer) Mattias Strand (Programmer) Tindra Zetterström (Programmer & 2D Artist) Mathildra Ulfves (Sound & Music) Jakob Hansen (Sound & Music) Hentriette Flach (Sound & Music) Olov Bladin (Sound & Music) Audio Engine: FMOD Studio by Firelight Technologies Pty Ltd.
